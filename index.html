Log-Periodic Antenna for THz Applications

2024 – Present | Supervised by Prof. Santanu Manna

Designing, simulating, and fabricating planar log-periodic antennas optimized for terahertz frequencies (0.1-1 THz) for next-generation wireless communications and sensing applications. The research involves electromagnetic simulation using HFSS, cleanroom fabrication processes, and comprehensive device characterization.

Key Achievements:
• Designed wideband antenna with 15% improved radiation efficiency
• Successfully fabricated prototypes in IIT Delhi cleanroom
• Achieved broadband operation covering 0.1-1 THz range
• Contributing to journal publication on THz antenna design
