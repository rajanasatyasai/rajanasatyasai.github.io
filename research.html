PRIMARY RESEARCH PROJECT

Log-Periodic Antenna for THz Applications

2024–Present Prof. Santanu Manna IIT Delhi

Objective

Design, simulate, fabricate, and characterize planar log-periodic antennas optimized for terahertz frequency range (0.1-1 THz) for next-generation wireless communication and sensing applications.

Research Approach
EM Simulation: Parametric design optimization using Ansys HFSS and CST Microwave Studio
Fabrication: Cleanroom processing including photolithography, metal deposition (e-beam evaporation), and lift-off techniques
Characterization: S-parameter measurements, radiation pattern analysis, and THz time-domain spectroscopy
Key Achievements
Designed wideband antenna covering 0.1-1 THz with VSWR < 2
Achieved 15% improvement in radiation efficiency through geometric optimization
Successfully fabricated prototypes in IIT Delhi cleanroom facility
Integration with on-chip THz sources for compact system demonstration
Applications: 6G wireless communications, THz imaging, spectroscopy, non-destructive testing
