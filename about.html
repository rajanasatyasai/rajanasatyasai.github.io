Professional Summary

Overview of my expertise and research focus

I am a dedicated researcher and engineer specializing in semiconductor device fabrication, terahertz (THz) antenna design, and quantum computing applications. Currently pursuing MS(Research) at IIT Delhi, my work focuses on designing and fabricating log-periodic antennas for THz frequency applications, combining electromagnetic simulation, cleanroom processing, and advanced characterization techniques.

With a strong foundation in electrical engineering from SASTRA University and industry experience at Tata Consultancy Services, I bring a unique blend of academic rigor and practical problem-solving skills. My research interests span THz devices, semiconductor fabrication, quantum electronics, VLSI design, and the emerging field of quantum computing.

As a teaching assistant at IIT Delhi, I am passionate about education and mentoring the next generation of engineers. I am also an active member of the quantum computing community as an IBM Qiskit Advocate, contributing to open-source projects and educational initiatives.
